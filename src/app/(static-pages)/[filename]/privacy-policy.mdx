4.1. The processing of Customers' personal data is based on:

5.1. Customers' personal data will be stored for the period necessary to achieve the purposes for which they were collected, as well as to fulfill obligations arising from legal provisions.

6.1. Customers have the right to:

Access to your personal data,
Correcting your personal data,
Deleting your personal data (unless there are grounds for further processing),
Restrictions on data processing,
Transferring data to another administrator,
Filing a complaint with the supervisory authority.

7.1. The Administrator takes all technical and organizational measures to protect Customers' personal data against loss, unauthorized access, change or disclosure.
